My Sporting Journey

Achievements on the Field and Track

KTU University Football Team

2023-24 | SRM University, Chennai

I was proud to represent the Kerala Technical University (KTU) as a member of the official football team. Playing in the demanding position of Right Wing-Back, I was responsible for both defensive solidity and contributing to our forward attacks. The experience culminated in our participation in a prestigious tournament at SRM University, Chennai, which was a true test of our skill, teamwork, and resilience.

3x College Athletics Champion

Thejus Engineering College

Athletics has always been a core part of my identity. During my time at Thejus Engineering College, I was honored to be named the Individual Athletics Champion for three consecutive years. My success was built on versatility, competing and excelling in the demanding middle-distance 1500m and 800m races, as well as the precision-based High Jump event. This achievement reflects my dedication and passion for all-around athleticism.

E-Zone Volleyball Champions

First Place Victory

As a dedicated member of our college team, we successfully clinched the title of E-Zone Volleyball Champions. The tournament, hosted at Sreepathy Engineering College, was a showcase of our team's strategy and coordination. This victory earned us the prestigious opportunity to advance and compete in the Inter-Zone Volleyball Championship, representing our zone against top-tier opponents. ]
